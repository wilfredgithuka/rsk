The Radio Society of Kenya

The Radio Society of Kenya (RSK) seeks to promote & develop amateur (Ham) radio to advance the art & science of radio & its public service role in Kenya, and is open to all those interested. The society continues to grow from strength to strength and is in an exciting stage of growth as we embrace new technologies and operational structures.
