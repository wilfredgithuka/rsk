The Radio Society of Kenya

Radio Society of Kenya (RSK) meets monthly at the shack, Joseph Kangethe Road, Woodley Estate, Nairobi County, Kenya. The 2020 Annual General Meeting was held on 11th January, 2020, at Planning House, Lower Kabete Road, Nairobi County, Kenya. The information shared is intended to inform visitors of the presence and activities of amateur radio in Kenya and the region. Karibu!! (welcome)
